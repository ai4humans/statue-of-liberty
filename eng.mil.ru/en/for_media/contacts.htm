Procedure of work with mass media

Media should send an application to Department of Media Affairs and Information of Ministry of Defence of the Russian Federation to visit military units and organizations for conducting information events.

Please, indicate following data in application addressed to Chief of Department of Media Affairs and Information of Ministry of Defence of the Russian Federation:

  • list of questions in interview (topics, subjects, scenes);
  • terms of conducting of informational event, list of media representatives (for foreign media: indicate accreditation cards numbers, issued in procedure established by Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Russian Federation);
  • list of technical equipment (systems) to be used;
  • general information about media.

The application form is provided by the editorial office and should be signed by Editor-in-Chief (or Deputy).
